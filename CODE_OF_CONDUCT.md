# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors. We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on the work and ideas, not personal criticism
- Help maintain a positive learning environment

## Expected Behavior

- Be inclusive of different perspectives and experiences
- Communicate clearly and courteously
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on collaboration and learning

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior
- Disrespectful language or personal attacks
- Unwelcome attention or advances
- Any form of abuse

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. Instances of unacceptable behavior may result in removal from the project.

## Contact

For concerns or violations, please contact the project maintainers directly.
